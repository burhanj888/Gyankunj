"The first five years have so much to do with how the next 80 turn out."

- Bill Gates

The brain of a child processes and remembers maximum amount of information that it receives during the first five years. This is the reason why it is of utmost importance to provide our children with a controlled environment where they can learn fearlessly and naturally. With love and compassion at the heart, we aim to create a fundamentally sound plinth for our children.

Vision

“To empower our children by nurturing a home like environment where they can learn naturally, fearlessly and actively, while enjoying each and every learning experience in a playful and fun filled manner.”

Mission

"With a child centric approach at the heart of each activity, we aim to make the first few years of our children as fulfilling as possible by turning each learning activity into a game where our children can play, enjoy and gain confidence to hone as well as showcase their inner abilities and talent in a friendly and compassionate environment.”

Values

  • Fostering a healthy environment for the child to learn freely and actively.
  • "Love, care and share" is our fundamental principle.
  • Every child is different, thus using range of different learning activities to understand the potential of every child.
  • Playing, singing, dancing, drawing and painting activities to help the children in learning naturally.
  • Social interaction with teachers and classmates, to help the children in developing their understanding of the world.
